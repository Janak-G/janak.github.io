1. Open Chrome

  2. Search for any image(For eg.Rose)

  3. Click on that image

    4. A list of images will appear & click on one of the image

  4. Hold the image

    5. After that a new site on your selected image will open where your selected image will be on the top. Hold that image

  5. Click on open image in new tab

    6. After you hold the image, a new dialogue box will appear. In that dialogue box, click on the option "Open image in new tab"


  6. Access the tab & copy the link

    7. After that, on the top right corner, you will find a square with a number written on it near the three dots, that is know as "TABS". Click on it & then you will find a new tab opened. Click that tab & copy the link of that tab.


    Return home
